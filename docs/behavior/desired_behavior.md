# Desired System Behavior

## Core Objective
Identify and prioritize arbitrage opportunities between Goodwill online auctions and eBay resale market through automated analysis and human-approved suggestions.

## Primary Behaviors

### 1. Data Collection
- **Goodwill Scraping**: Continuously monitor shopgoodwill.com for new listings
- **eBay Price Intelligence**: Gather sold/completed listing data via eBay API
- **Market Research**: Track pricing trends and demand patterns

### 2. Opportunity Identification
- **Undervalued Items**: Detect items listed below market value
- **Mislabeled Products**: Identify luxury/high-value items incorrectly categorized
- **Timing Arbitrage**: Find auctions ending at low-competition times
- **Geographic Arbitrage**: Exploit regional pricing differences

### 3. Analysis Pipeline
- **LLM Analysis**: Natural language processing of item descriptions
- **Image AI**: Computer vision for product identification and condition assessment
- **Market Comparison**: Real-time price comparison across platforms
- **Authenticity Assessment**: Risk scoring for counterfeit likelihood

### 4. Output Generation
- **Suggestion Files**: Ranked opportunities with evidence and confidence scores
- **Human Approval Workflow**: Present findings for manual review and decision
- **Automated Actions**: Execute approved purchases/bids within defined parameters

## Success Metrics
- **Profit Margin**: Target 50-200% profit margin on resale
- **Success Rate**: >70% of suggestions result in profitable sales
- **Time to Market**: List items on eBay within 48 hours of purchase
- **ROI**: Achieve positive ROI within 30 days per item

## Risk Management
- **Budget Limits**: Never exceed predefined spending limits
- **Category Restrictions**: Focus on low-risk, high-value categories
- **Quality Assurance**: Maintain high seller rating through accurate descriptions
